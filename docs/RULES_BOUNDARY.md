# Rules Boundary

PorchQuest369 is a fantasy tabletop-inspired RPG engine. It should not claim to be official Dungeons & Dragons, and it should not use protected trademarks, logos, settings, characters, art, or non-open content.

Safe public wording:

- prompt-based RPG engine
- fantasy tabletop-inspired
- 5e-light inspired rules
- SRD-compatible adapter, when attribution and license terms are handled

Avoid in repo branding:

- official D&D logo/name as the project title
- Forgotten Realms, Ravenloft, Eberron, Dragonlance, or other protected settings unless separately licensed
- protected monsters, spells, art, trade dress, or product identity outside a verified open rules source

The default campaign is original: **Lanterns Under Blackwood Hill**.
